How to Play

SmashJoy is an addictive stickman army-building game where you collect fighters and battle against enemy groups. Swipe to control your stickman army, collect number gates to grow your forces, and strategically navigate through obstacles to reach the finish line.

With simple controls and exciting gameplay, SmashJoy offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

Game Features

  • Easy to learn, one-finger controls
  • Collect and grow your stickman army
  • Battle against enemy forces
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Vibrant, colorful graphics
  • Exciting boss battles

Tips & Strategies

Collect Wisely: Always aim for the highest number gates to maximize your army size.

Avoid Obstacles: Be careful with negative number gates and obstacles that can reduce your army.

Boss Battles: Save as many stickmen as possible for the final boss battle at the end of each level.

Strategic Positioning: Position your army to collect bonuses while avoiding traps and enemies.
